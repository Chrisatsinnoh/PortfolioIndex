About

What is your project about, exactly?

My project is about making a video game with the genres of Life Simulation and Sandbox combined all-together. It's also the main inspiration of 2 life simulation games and it will also incorporate a slice of a promotion that used to be held by Noel Cookies Company S.A.S. in 2009. At this point, nothing much is revealed about my project except that I have concept artwork of most of the worlds at bay and made 2016 redesigns of the characters that were involved in the said 2009 promotion.

What should I expect from your project?

I except my game to have a rating between E to E+10 as I have the aim of making it family-friendly. It will be released on PC on Steam and if a popular demand is met, it's going to be available on Playstation 4 and XBOX One only. I will not release the game for the Nintendo Switch.

How can I contribute to your project?

If you want to volunteer for my project, you can contact me via email and you must accomplish the following requirements in order to be selected as a proper volunteer for my project: 1.- You must be 18 and over if you want to be paid for the project, or 14 and over if you want to be an unpaid one. 2.- You must have a Skill Level on what volunteer act you are going to be involved with on a level between 7 to 10. 3.- You must understand the Volunteer Guidelines before proceding. 4.- You must show me examples of your work in order to determine wether or not you get the opportunity to volunteer.
Who are you? Tell me more about you.
My name is Christian Soares Jesus Zaccaro. I was born on October 23rd on a country called Venezuela. After graduating high school with a science honor, I pursued my goals on becoming a game developing by honing my project's plans at Stetson University with a Digital Arts major. I took classes from Digital Art Fundamentals to Web Design. Below is a set of skills that I have regarding traditional art (like drawing) and digital art, in case if I get hired for other projects that I can put most of my time to work on, mid-project or after my project is complete. 1.- Drawing (Concept art and Character Design): 8 out of 10 Skill Points 2.- Drawing (Environment and Building Design): 4 out of 10 Skill Points 3.- Digital Art (Graphic Design and Promotional Art): 5 out of 10 Skill Points 4.- Coding (Computing): 1 out of 10 Skill Points 5.- Digital Audio (Composing Music and Audio): 4 out of 10 Skill Points For business inquiries regarding those 5 Skill Areas, please contact me on the Contact Tab.
