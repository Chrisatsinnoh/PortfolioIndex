Characters

Although the star of the show will be yourself, or a character that you can create on your own, there's several characters to interact with and even play as them. They all vary in personality and in other backgrounds!

Worlds

There are several worlds to live in or explore. Take a tropical getaway at Coral Reef Islands. Find yourself in a misty, mysterious town in the heart of Malleywell Hollows, or have a suburban, natural living at Bluegables Valley Vale.

Features and More

From the Designer Pad 3000 to the many events happening in each world, my project will include several features that will improve the game's quality in the interaction section.
