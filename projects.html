Inspirations and Varieties

My project will be inspired by Life Simulation, Sandbox and Adventure Games. I can't reveal the name of those games yet until half my project is complete. My game will of course have variety to make my project unique and stand out to other projects. For example, the way a player can design a house is by using a tablet, where all the furniture and wallpapers are stored, and load pre-made interiors or make a new one from scratch. Events may vary in the worlds that I will release for the base game, and the player can pick a set of events based on a different country or make a new set of events. All of these features will serve as an example of how my game will have its own unique points to make it different or similar than other games in the video game industry.

Other information

Since the name of my game is going to be Worfelia, people can opt to have the name of the game changed if it doesn't fit. I may depend on the community to ask them various questions on what they want to see on the game; like how the intro cutscenes of moving in to a world for the first time should look like, the staff credits playing in-game (and of course, carry on playing after the credits), and other things. There are many aspects of my game that does not require change and look good for me or I can make a change to these aspects to make them a good fit for my ideal image, but when I'm stumped on what to do on the remaining features that leave me with a doubt, I can depend on the community to help me.
